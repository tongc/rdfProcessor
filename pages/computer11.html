Apple MacBook Pro 13" with Retina Display

The new Apple MacBook Pro ME864B/A Retina 13" Laptop comes with the stunning new Retina display, promising the clearest visuals you could hope for to match this laptop's outstanding performance. Driven by a super fast 4th generation Intel® i5 processor and running the latest Mac OS X Mavericks operating system, you can enjoy seamless computing and lightning fast operating speeds.

Retina display

The unique Retina display has 5.1 million pixels and delivers remarkably vivid and detailed images. This 13” LED display offers a 2560 x 1600 resolution, revealing more of your high-resolution images onscreen with pixel-for-pixel accuracy. The new Retina display also delivers a wide viewing angle thanks to IPS technology, reduced glare and formidable colours. To compliment this great display, the new MacBook Pro ME684B/A comes with Intel Iris Pro graphics processor for brilliantly fluid images.

Flash storage Building from the foundations up, this MacBook Pro uses flash at its roots. This makes it more durable, reliable and efficient, and above all, exceptionally fast. With 4 GB of RAM and a 128 GB SSD, you have high-performance storage at your disposal.

Thunderbolt This impressive laptop also sports two of Apple's innovative, blisteringly fast Thunderbolt 2 ports, which offer input/output speeds which, at up to 10Gbps, are more than 20 times faster than USB 2.0 and 12 times faster than FireWire 800. It can deliver support for video and eight-channel audio and easily hook up with HDMI-compatible devices via an adapter.

Maverick OS X Mavericks offers so much with full screen apps through the Launchpad desktop, AirDrop which allows you to share files wirelessly without the need for WiFi, and Version, which takes screenshots of your documents, showing you different versions of the document as it is has progressed.

Connected When you are not making the most of this laptop's graphics for work, you can use it to chat on the internet via the HD webcam, or to surf your favourite sites. With WiFi connectivity all you need is a wireless hotspot and the web is yours to enjoy. In addition to all this, the Apple MacBook Pro ME864B/A Retina 13" Laptop offers super fast start up, a remarkable battery life of up to 9 hours, an effortless multi-touch trackpad and many more great Mac features you'll enjoy discovering!
